My Journey

I'll Start By Talking About How I Got To Where I Am,So I Finished My Studies In The Middle East Due To Fathers Profession,I Never Knew Much About Tech As I Didn't Really Understand How Tech Was Used Due To Limited Resorces And Education. I Pretty Much Had Everything Planned Out By My Father, He Decided For Me To Become A Doctor Like Himself.I Didn't Really Know What I Wanted To Do So I Decided To Go Ahead With It.Sometime Later When I Got The Admission And It Was Close By That I Had To Leave To Study. I Felt A Strong Feeling Of Dissatisfaction.I Couldn't See Myself In The Next 6 Years As A Doctor, It Just Wasn't Me.I Explained It To My Father And He Understood My Decision As I Didn't Have Much Interest For It. I Didn't Know What I Wanted To Do, Tech Always Seemed Very Interesting However It Was Very Complex To Understand At The Start.Coding Seemed Amazing As I Could Visualise Something And Code It.I Watched Many Tutorials And Hired An Online Teacher To Help Me Understand Coding As It Was Something I Wanted To Get In To.
